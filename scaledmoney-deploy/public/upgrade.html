Frequently Asked

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account settings whenever you want. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period. No questions, no penalties.
What payment methods do you accept?
We use Stripe for secure payments. All major credit and debit cards are accepted. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.
What happens to my data if I downgrade?
Your data stays safe. If you downgrade to Free, you keep access to Core tools and any sprints you have created. Pro-only tools become read-only.
Is there a free trial?
The Free tier is your trial — use it as long as you want. When you are ready for the full toolkit, upgrade. No pressure.
Do I need a credit card to start?
No. The Free tier requires no credit card. You only enter payment info when you choose to upgrade to Pro.
